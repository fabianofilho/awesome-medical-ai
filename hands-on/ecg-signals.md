# Mão na Massa — ECG e Sinais

- [DeepPSP/torch_ecg](https://github.com/DeepPSP/torch_ecg) — Modelos de DL para ECG em PyTorch com ferramentas de processamento de sinais.
- [eddymina/ECG_Classification_Pytorch](https://github.com/eddymina/ECG_Classification_Pytorch) — Redes convolucionais 1D para classificação de ECG.
- [cepdnaclk/Comprehensive-ECG-analysis-with-Deep-Learning](https://github.com/cepdnaclk/e17-4yp-Comprehensive-ECG-analysis-with-Deep-Learning-on-GPU-accelerators) — Análise abrangente de ECG com DL em GPUs.
- [rPPG-Toolbox](https://github.com/ubicomplab/rPPG-Toolbox) — Medição remota de sinais fisiológicos via câmera. (NeurIPS 2023)
