# Papers — ECG e Sinais Biológicos

- [MEETI: A Multimodal ECG Dataset from MIMIC-IV-ECG with Signals, Images, Features and Interpretations (2026)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-026-06796-1) — Primeiro dataset de grande escala sincronizando ECG bruto, imagens de alta resolução e interpretações textuais detalhadas com LLMs. (Scientific Data, Mai 2026)
- [Multimodal Transformer-Based Electrocardiogram Analysis for Cardiovascular Comorbidity Detection (2026)](https://formative.jmir.org/2026/1/e80815) — Transformers multimodais para análise de ECG integrando sinais, métricas estruturadas (PR/QRS/QT) e demografia. (JMIR Formative Research, 2026)
- [Decoding the language of sleep with artificial intelligence (2026)](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736(26)00375-2/abstract) — Como a IA pode transformar o sono em dado médico acionável. (The Lancet)
- [Cardiologist-level arrhythmia detection and classification in ambulatory ECGs (2019)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-018-0268-3) — Detecção de arritmias usando DNN. (Nature Medicine)
- [Deep learning prediction of all-cause mortality from 12-lead ECGs (2020)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-020-0870-z) — Predição de mortalidade a partir de ECG. (Nature Medicine)
- [Automatic diagnosis of the 12-lead ECG using a deep neural network (2020)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-020-15432-4) — Diagnóstico automático de ECG. (Nature Communications)
- [An artificial intelligence-enabled ECG algorithm for identification of atrial fibrillation (2019)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-018-0339-5) — Identificar FA a partir de ECG em ritmo sinusal. (Nature Medicine)
